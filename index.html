Course Overview

This course explores cutting-edge generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) technologies that are revolutionizing how we create and manipulate digital content across multiple domains - from images and videos to text, code, music, and even molecular structures. This advanced-level course is designed to be seminar-based and research-driven. Instead of teaching basic skills in machine learning and artificial intelligence, this course aims to help you understand state-of-the-art techniques in GenAI.
